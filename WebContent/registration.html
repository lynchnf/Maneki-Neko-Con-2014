You are purchasing a one-year supporting membership to the non-profit organization, Lucky Cat Educational Enterprises, LLC. This entitles you to attend any and all sponsored events hosted by LCEE which includes the annual Maneki Neko Convention. Supporting members are reminded to follow all rules for said events for the enjoyment and safety of all attendees or membership rights may be revoked.

The Maneki Neko Convention is now a THREE (3) day event!! Everyone with a valid badge for the event will have access to:

  • ALL gaming (video arcade, Card game tournament, Nerf tournament, Board games, RPGs, etc.)
  • ALL programming panels
  • ALL special guests
  • ALL vendors & artists
  • ALL costume contests
  • The Pizza PJ Party
  • The Art Show
  • and MORE!

Your badge is your proof of membership and acts as your admission to the event. Your badge must be visible to staff at all times while at the convention venue. You may hide the badge from view only temporarily and only during photographs. (We want you to take many pictures of yourself and any others who are willing to be photographed while at Maneki Neko Con so have fun!)

Claiming your Badge

Please bring a photo ID with you (school, work, or government-issue) to claim your pre-reg badge or to register at the door. Minors may have their parent or legal guardian vouch for their identity.

If you are a student you must show current Student ID from your school or if you are military personell you must show a military ID to claim your discounted badge. If valid ID is not provided, a $5 surcharge may be added at the door.

Minors under the age of 13 years old (i.e. 12-and-under) may register for a badge at-the-door for free, but must be accompanied by a paid badge-holding adult/responsible party at all times.

If you will be attending in costume (which we encourage) then please make sure we can see your face and validate your ID during registration at the con.

Please contact us with any registration-related questions at reg @ maneki-neko-con.org
