Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to bring photo identification to register for Maneki Neko Con?

A: If you pre-registered, you MUST bring a photo id to pick up your badge.
If you have bought or are buyinig a discounted student/military badge, you MUST bring your student/military id or you will have to pay an additionial $5.
If you are getting a Kid-In-Tow badge, you MUST bring the kid (12 years or under) and the accompanying adult.


Q: Where is Maneki Neko Con?

A: At the Matteson Community Center at 20642 Matteson Ave, Matteson, IL 60443


Q: Can I get to the Matteson Community Center using public transit?

A: Yes. Check out the RTA Trip Planner for details.


Q: Is smoking allowed at the Matteson Community Center?

A: No. The Matteson Community Center has a policy against smoking anywhere on the site. Smokers will have to go off site or across the street to smoke.
